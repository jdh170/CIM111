ONE DIRECTION

2010 2011 2012 2013 2014 2015

This is a timeline of the band One Direction.

2010

One Direction began their career on the hit UK show, The X Factor. All members auditioned individually and were joined together by Simon Cowell. They placed 3rd in the competition before they were voted off, but that didn't stop them.

2011

The boys continued their group outside of the show and released their first single "What Makes You Beautiful" in 2011. It was a number one hit for weeks and subsequently led to thier signing with Columbia Records that November. Within that month, the band released their debut album, Up All Night, to the UK and England.

2012

The lads proceeded to go on their first headlining UK tour in January 2012. They eventually became the opening act for America's boy band, Big Time Rush, and toured North America for the first time.Up All Night was released in March on an international basis. It reached number one on the USA charts, and was inducted into the Guinness World Records for being the first UK album to do so.

2013

Towards the end of 2012, 1D released their second studio album, Take Me Home. It was a smash and the boys took off on their second tour in 2013. For both tours, the band recieved acclaim for their high-quality live vocals and stage presence. A documentary called, One Direction: This Is Us, was filmed during the tour and captured their lives on and off the stage. The band globally released their third album, Midnight Memories, in November of the same year.

2014

In April, the band launched their first all-stadium tour; the Where We Are tour. They proceeded to release their fourth studio album in November titled, Four, and launched their second stadium tour. With only two months left of the tour, Zayn Malik decided the leave the group. The remaining band members decided to stay together and continue the group as a foursome.

2015

With the band having gone from five guys to four, Harry, Louis, Niall, and Liam joined togetner and released Made in the A.M., which marks the group's fifth, and last, studio album. Despite having recieved multiple accolades for the project, the band announced their hiatus at the end of 2015. It was supposed to be an 18-month break, but since then each member has released individual music. Unfortunately for us Directioners, it looks like the band is done for good.
